Install

Put it on your home screen. It behaves like one.

Vull is a Progressive Web App — one install, and it opens full-screen, works offline for editing, and shows up next to your other apps.

1

Open the editor

Visit the app in Chrome, Edge, or Safari — desktop or mobile.

2

Tap "Install" or "Add to Home Screen"

Desktop Chrome shows an install icon in the address bar. On iPhone, use Share → Add to Home Screen.

3

Launch it like any app

It opens in its own window, no browser chrome, with an icon on your dock, taskbar, or home screen.
